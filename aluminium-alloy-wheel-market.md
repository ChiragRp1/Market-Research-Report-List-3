<p><p>The Aluminium Alloy Wheel market can be categorized into three types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves the process of pouring molten aluminum into a mold to create the wheel shape. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping the aluminum through the application of heat and pressure. The "other" category encompasses various wheel manufacturing methods that do not fall strictly under casting or forging, such as flow forming or billet machining. These different types offer unique characteristics and performance attributes for consumers to choose from.</p></p>
