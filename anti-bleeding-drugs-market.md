<p><p>Anti-bleeding drugs market includes Collagen, Oxidized Regenerated Cellulose, Gelatin, and Polysaccharide types. Collagen-based products are derived from animal sources and promote blood clotting. Oxidized Regenerated Cellulose is a plant-based product that enhances clotting by forming a gel-like barrier. Gelatin-based products work by absorbing water and forming a gel to promote clotting. Polysaccharide-based products help in forming a clot by promoting platelet aggregation. Each type of anti-bleeding drug has unique mechanisms of action to control and prevent excessive bleeding.</p></p>
