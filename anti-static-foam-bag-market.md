High-density polyethylene (HDPE) foam bags offer strong impact resistance, while middle-density polyethylene (MDPE) provides a good balance of cushioning and flexibility. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) foam bags offer high clarity and flexibility, while linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) foam bags provide excellent puncture resistance and tear strength. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
