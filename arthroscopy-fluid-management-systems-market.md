<p><p>Arthroscopy fluid management systems are used during arthroscopic surgeries to maintain clear visibility by irrigating and suctioning the joint. There are two main types of disposable systems available: pump management disposables and gravity management disposables. Pump management disposables use a pump to control the flow rate of irrigation fluid, while gravity management disposables rely on gravity to regulate the flow. Both systems are essential for efficient fluid management during arthroscopic procedures, ensuring optimal visualization and successful surgical outcomes.</p></p>
