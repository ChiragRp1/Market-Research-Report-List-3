<p><p>The Artificial Pancreas Device System (APDS) market consists of three main types: Threshold Suspended Device Systems, Hybrid Closed-loop APDS, and Other Systems. Threshold Suspended Device Systems monitor glucose levels and automatically suspend insulin delivery when a pre-set low threshold is reached. Hybrid Closed-loop APDS automatically adjust insulin delivery based on real-time glucose levels. Other Systems include innovative technologies that aim to improve diabetes management, such as predictive algorithms and personalized insulin dosing recommendations. Each type offers unique benefits to patients with diabetes.</p></p>
