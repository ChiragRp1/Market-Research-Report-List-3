<p><p>Automated overhead cranes are used in industrial settings for efficient material handling. These cranes can also be classified based on their type: single-girder and double-girder overhead cranes. Single-girder cranes have one bridge girder, while double-girder cranes have two, offering increased lifting capacity. Each of these market segments plays a crucial role in various industries' material handling operations.</p></p>
