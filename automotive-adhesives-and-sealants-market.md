<p><p>Automotive adhesives and sealants market consists of two main types: adhesives and sealants. Adhesives are used to bond or join different materials together in vehicles, while sealants are used to fill gaps and prevent leakage of fluids. Adhesives provide high strength bonding for components like trim, panels, and structural parts. Sealants are used for sealing joints, gaps, and seams to protect against moisture, dust, and corrosion. Both adhesives and sealants play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle performance, durability, and aesthetics.</p></p>
