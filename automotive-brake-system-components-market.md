<p><p>The automotive brake system & components market includes various types of braking systems such as brake booster, master cylinder, drum brake, and disc brake. Brake boosters use vacuum pressure to assist with brake pedal force, while master cylinders convert mechanical force into hydraulic pressure. Drum brakes use brake shoes to apply friction against a drum, while disc brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a rotating disc. Each component plays a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient braking performance in vehicles.</p></p>
