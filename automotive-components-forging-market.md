<p><strong>The Automotive Components Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive components forging market includes various types such as closed die forgings, open die forgings, and rolled rings forgings. Closed die forgings involve forcing metal into a die to create a desired shape, producing high strength parts. Open die forgings use hammers or presses to shape metal without confining molds, leading to unique and customizable designs. Rolled rings forgings involve shaping metal through rolling and forging processes to create seamless rings with superior strength and durability, ideal for automotive applications.</p></p>
