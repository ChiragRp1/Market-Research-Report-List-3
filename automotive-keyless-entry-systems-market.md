<p><p>Automotive Keyless Entry Systems come in different types, such as Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) markets. RFID keyless entry systems use radio frequency signals to automatically unlock and lock the car doors, providing convenience for the users. On the other hand, BLE keyless entry systems use Bluetooth technology to connect with the car, allowing users to unlock or start the vehicle from a distance. These technologies offer secure and efficient ways to access and control vehicles without the need for physical keys.</p></p>
