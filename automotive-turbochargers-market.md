<p><p>Automotive turbochargers can be categorized into two main types: Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo. Mono Turbo systems consist of a single turbocharger that uses exhaust gases to increase engine power. On the other hand, Twin Turbo systems utilize two turbochargers; one is smaller and responds quickly at lower engine speeds, while the second larger turbo becomes active at higher speeds. Both types are designed to improve engine performance and efficiency by increasing air intake, ultimately enhancing overall vehicle power and acceleration.</p></p>
