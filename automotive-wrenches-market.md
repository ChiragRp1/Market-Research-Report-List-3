<p><p>The automotive wrenches market consists of various types of wrenches that cater to different needs. Open end wrenches are designed with two open ends to fit different sizes of nuts and bolts. Box end wrenches have a closed loop on one or both ends for a firmer grip. Combination wrenches have one open end and one box end, providing versatility. Pipe wrenches are specifically designed for gripping and turning pipes. Other types include adjustable wrenches, torque wrenches, and socket wrenches, each serving a specific purpose in automotive repair and maintenance.</p></p>
