<p><p>Bitumen additives market consists of various types such as emulsifiers, polymeric modifiers, anti-strip and adhesion promoters, and others. Emulsifiers are used to facilitate the mixing of bitumen with water, polymeric modifiers improve the performance and durability of asphalt, anti-strip additives prevent moisture-induced damage, and adhesion promoters enhance the bond between bitumen and aggregates. Other additives may include rejuvenators, colorants, and anti-oxidants. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the overall quality and performance of asphalt materials.</p></p>
