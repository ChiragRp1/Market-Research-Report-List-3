<p><p>Boom lifts are elevated platforms used for construction, maintenance, and repair work. Telescopic boom lifts have straight arms that extend vertically, while articulating boom lifts have jointed arms that can reach over obstacles. Trailer Mounted Boom Lifts are mobile units that can be towed behind a vehicle. Bucket trucks feature a bucket or platform attached to a hydraulic arm for aerial work. Towable boom lifts are compact units that can be easily transported to different job sites. Each type of boom lift serves a specific purpose in the market.</p></p>
