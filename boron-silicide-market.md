<p><p>Boron silicide is a ceramic compound that is composed of boron and silicon. It is commonly referred to as boron monosilicide or boron silicon monoide. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for boron silicide in the automotive industry for manufacturing high-performance components and advanced electronic systems. Boron silicide is used as a coating material in automotive components like brake discs, clutch plates, and turbochargers due to its excellent thermal and chemical stability.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for boron silicide in the aerospace and defense sectors is further driving market growth. Boron silicide is utilized in the production of specialized ceramic composites and thermal protection systems for spacecraft and missiles due to its exceptional high-temperature resistance and low density.</p><p>Moreover, the expanding energy sector, particularly in developing countries, is creating new opportunities for the boron silicide market.
