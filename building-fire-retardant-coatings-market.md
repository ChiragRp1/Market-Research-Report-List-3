<p><p>Building fire retardant coatings are used to reduce the spread of flames in case of fire, protecting the structure and allowing more time for evacuation. There are two main types of market for these coatings: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and have low VOC levels. Solvent-based coatings are more durable and provide stronger adhesion to surfaces. Both types of coatings offer effective fire protection for various types of buildings and structures.</p></p>
