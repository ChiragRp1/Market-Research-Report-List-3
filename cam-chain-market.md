<p><ul><li>Roller Chain</li><li>Silent Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cam chain market can be classified into roller chain and silent chain. Roller chain utilizes rollers to reduce friction and improve efficiency, making it ideal for high-speed applications. On the other hand, silent chain offers a smoother and quieter operation, making it suitable for applications where noise is a concern. Both types of chains are commonly used in automotive engines to drive the camshaft and maintain precise timing, ensuring optimal performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
