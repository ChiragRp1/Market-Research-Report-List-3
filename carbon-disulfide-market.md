<p><p>Carbon Disulfide market can be classified into two main types: Natural Gas Method Carbon Disulfide and Coke Method Carbon Disulfide. Natural Gas Method Carbon Disulfide is produced by reacting methane with sulfur vapor at high temperatures, while Coke Method Carbon Disulfide is produced by reacting coke (a type of coal) with sulfur at high temperatures. Both methods have their own advantages and disadvantages, leading to varying market demands and prices for the two types of Carbon Disulfide.</p></p>
