<p><p>Cerebral Spinal Fluid (CSF) Management market includes CSF Shunts and CSF Drainage devices. CSF Shunts are used to treat hydrocephalus by diverting excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body. CSF Drainage devices are used for temporary drainage of CSF in cases like traumatic brain injury or intracranial hemorrhage. Both types of devices are essential in managing CSF flow and pressure in patients with various neurological conditions.</p></p>
