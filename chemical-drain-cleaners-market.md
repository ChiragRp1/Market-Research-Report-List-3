<p><p>Chemical drain cleaners are categorized into two types based on their chemical composition - Alkaline Type and Acidic Type. Alkaline drain cleaners typically contain ingredients like sodium hydroxide or potassium hydroxide, which work by emulsifying grease and breaking down organic matter in drains. Acidic drain cleaners, on the other hand, use chemicals like sulfuric acid or hydrochloric acid to dissolve clogs caused by hair, soap scum, and other substances. Both types are effective at clearing blockages in drains, but they may have different applications and safety precautions.</p></p>
