<p><strong>The Chloroprene Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acetylene Method</li><li>Butadiene Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chloroprene Rubber is produced through two main methods: Acetylene Method and Butadiene Method. Acetylene Method involves reacting acetylene with hydrochloric acid to produce chloroprene monomer, which is then polymerized to form chloroprene rubber. On the other hand, Butadiene Method involves using butadiene as a starting material for the production of chloroprene rubber. Both methods result in the manufacturing of high-quality chloroprene rubber, which is widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and adhesives.</p></p>
