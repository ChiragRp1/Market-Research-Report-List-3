<p><ul><li>Acid Resistant</li><li>Alkali Resistant</li><li>Acid and Alkali Resistant Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cleaner dyes can be categorized into different types based on their resistance to acid, alkali, or both. Acid resistant dyes are specifically designed to maintain their color and integrity when exposed to acidic substances. Alkali resistant dyes, on the other hand, are formulated to withstand alkaline environments without fading or deteriorating. Lastly, acid and alkali resistant dyes are versatile options that are capable of maintaining their properties in both acidic and alkaline conditions. These different types of cleaner dyes cater to various industrial and commercial applications that require specific resistance properties.</p></p>
