<p><p>The Climbing Gloves market is divided into two main types: Partial Finger and Full Finger. Partial Finger gloves cover the palm and lower part of the fingers, leaving the fingertips exposed for better grip and sensitivity. These gloves are often preferred for activities that require precision and dexterity. Full Finger gloves, on the other hand, provide complete coverage for the fingers, offering more protection from abrasions and impacts. They are commonly used in activities that involve rough and rugged surfaces.</p></p>
