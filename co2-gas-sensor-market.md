<p><p>CO2 gas sensors are classified into three main types based on their technology: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 gas sensors, Catalytic CO2 gas sensors, and Thermal Conductivity CO2 gas sensors. NDIR sensors use infrared light to detect CO2 levels, Catalytic sensors use a catalyst to measure CO2 concentration, and Thermal Conductivity sensors measure changes in thermal conductivity to determine CO2 presence. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in industries such as automotive, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
