<p><p>Coating protection films are categorized into three main types based on the material used: PVC (Polyvinyl chloride) type, PU (Polyurethane) type, and TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) type. PVC type films are durable and cost-effective, making them popular for mass production. PU type films offer greater flexibility and scratch resistance, ideal for high-end applications. TPU type films provide superior impact protection and clarity. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the coating protection film market.</p></p>
