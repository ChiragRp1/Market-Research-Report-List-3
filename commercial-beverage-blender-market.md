<p><p>The commercial beverage blender market consists of three main types: monofunctional blenders, undiluted liquid blenders, and multifunction blenders. Monofunctional blenders are designed specifically for blending fruits, vegetables, and ice to create beverages. Undiluted liquid blenders cater to the needs of bars and cafes, as they are specifically designed to blend undiluted liquids, such as juices and milkshakes. Multifunction blenders, on the other hand, offer a wider range of capabilities and can handle various tasks, including ice crushing, pureeing, and blending, making them suitable for different beverage preparation needs.</p></p>
