Steel piping is durable, strong, and widely used in industrial applications due to its high-pressure tolerance. Plastic piping, typically made of PVC or polyethylene, is cost-effective, easy to install, and perfect for low-pressure applications. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
