<p><strong>The Cosmetic Grade Polyvinyl Alcohol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Hydrolized</li><li>Partially Hydrolized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Polyvinyl Alcohol is categorized into two main types based on hydrolysis levels: Fully Hydrolized and Partially Hydrolized. Fully Hydrolized PVA is completely broken down, resulting in a high level of water solubility and film-forming properties. This type is often used in skincare products for its excellent barrier function and smooth texture. Partially Hydrolized PVA, on the other hand, has a lower degree of hydrolysis, making it more ideal for products that require a balance between water solubility and film-forming abilities, such as hair styling products.</p></p>
