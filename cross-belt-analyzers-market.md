<p><ul><li>PGNAA Technology</li><li>PFTNA Technology</li><li>Sodern Neutron Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross-Belt Analyzers are used in industries like mining and cement to quickly analyze materials on conveyor belts. There are three main types based on the technology used: PGNAA Technology, which uses gamma rays to measure elemental composition; PFTNA Technology, which uses prompt gamma neutron activation analysis to measure elemental composition; and Sodern Neutron Technology, which uses neutron sources to measure elemental composition. These analyzers provide real-time data to optimize production processes and ensure quality control.</p></p>
