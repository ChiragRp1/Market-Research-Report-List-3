<p><p>Current sensing transformers are used in various industries for measuring electric currents. There are three main types of current sensing transformers - Wound, Toroidal, and Bar. Wound transformers consist of a primary and secondary winding wrapped around a core, providing accurate current measurement. Toroidal transformers have a ring-like core that reduces electromagnetic interference and offers high efficiency. Bar transformers have a straight bar-shaped core, making them suitable for high current applications. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
