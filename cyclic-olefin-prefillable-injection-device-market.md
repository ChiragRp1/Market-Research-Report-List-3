<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Prefillable Injection Device is available in three main market types: Crystal Clear Polymer (CCP), Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP), and Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC). CCP is known for its excellent transparency and barrier properties, making it suitable for sensitive drug formulations. COP offers high heat resistance and chemical inertness, making it ideal for drug delivery devices. COC combines the advantages of both CCP and COP, offering a balance of transparency, heat resistance, and barrier properties for a wide range of drug delivery applications.</p></p>
