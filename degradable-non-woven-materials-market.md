<p><p>Degradable non-woven materials are categorized into different types based on their manufacturing process and properties. These types include spunlaced nonwovens, which are produced by entangling fibers using water jets; melt-blown nonwovens, made by extruding melted fibers; spunbond nonwovens, formed by bonding spun filaments together; needle-punched nonwovens, created by punching fibers with barbed needles. Other types of degradable non-woven materials may include air-laid and wet-laid nonwovens. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
