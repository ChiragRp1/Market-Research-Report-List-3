Solvent-based adhesives offer high strength and durability. Radiation-cured adhesives use ultraviolet or visible light to cure the adhesive quickly. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different dental applications based on the specific requirements of the procedure.</p></p>

Orthodontic bonding agents are used to attach brackets to teeth during orthodontic treatment. Luting cements are used to secure crowns and bridges. Tray adhesives help hold dental impression trays in place. Dental surgical tissue bonding is used to bond soft tissues together during dental procedures.
