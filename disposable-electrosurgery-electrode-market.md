<p><p>Disposable electrosurgery electrodes are used in surgical procedures for cutting, coagulation, and tissue removal. These electrodes come in two types: hand control and foot control. Hand control electrodes are operated directly by the surgeon, allowing for precise control and maneuverability during the procedure. Foot control electrodes, on the other hand, are operated by foot pedals, providing the surgeon with hands-free operation for increased efficiency and convenience. Both types of electrodes contribute to improved surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
