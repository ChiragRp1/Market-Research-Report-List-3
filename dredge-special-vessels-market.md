Cutter-suction Dredges use a rotating cutter head to loosen sediment before it is sucked up by a pump. Trailing Suction Dredges drag a suction pipe behind them to collect sediments. Bucket Dredgers use a bucket to scoop up sediment from the seabed. Each type of dredge vessel is designed for specific dredging tasks.</p></p>
