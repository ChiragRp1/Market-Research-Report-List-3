<p><p>Dry ice equipment market consists of two main types: dry ice production equipment and dry ice cleaning equipment. Dry ice production equipment is used to produce dry ice by cooling and compressing carbon dioxide (CO2) gas, which is then converted into solid dry ice pellets or blocks. Dry ice cleaning equipment, also known as CO2 blasting or dry ice blasting, uses compressed air to propel small dry ice pellets at high speeds to clean surfaces by removing dirt, grime, and other contaminants without leaving any residue behind.</p></p>
