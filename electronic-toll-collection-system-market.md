<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection System market consists of various types of systems. RFID-based ETC Systems utilize radio frequency identification technology to identify and charge vehicles. DSRC-based ETC Systems operate using short-range communication between vehicles and toll booths. Video analytic-based ETC Systems employ video cameras and image processing software to recognize and bill vehicles. GNSS/GPS-based ETC Systems use satellite-based navigation systems to track vehicles and apply toll charges. These different market types provide efficient and convenient methods for toll collection, ensuring smooth traffic flow and reduced congestion on roads.</p></p>
