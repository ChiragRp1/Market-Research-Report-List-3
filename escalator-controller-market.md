<p><ul><li>Non-proprietary Escalator Controllers</li><li>Proprietary Escalator Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-proprietary escalator controllers are those that can be used by any escalator manufacturer, providing flexibility and ease of integration for building owners and maintenance professionals. On the other hand, proprietary escalator controllers are specifically designed to work only with escalators from a particular manufacturer, limiting choices for building owners and potentially leading to higher costs for maintenance and repairs. The market for these two types of escalator controllers cater to different needs and preferences within the industry.</p></p>
