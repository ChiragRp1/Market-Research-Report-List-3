In the AlCl3 liquid hydrocarbon method, ethylbenzene is produced by reacting ethylene and benzene with aluminum chloride as a catalyst. The Zeolite gas catalytic method involves using zeolite catalysts to convert ethylene and benzene into ethylbenzene. The Zeolite liquid catalytic method is similar but uses a liquid form of zeolite catalyst. These methods are all utilized in the production of ethylbenzene for various industrial applications.</p></p>
