<p><ul><li>Exhaust Manifold Gaskets</li><li>Exhaust Flange Gaskets</li><li>Header Gaskets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust gaskets market is comprised of three main types: exhaust manifold gaskets, exhaust flange gaskets, and header gaskets. Exhaust manifold gaskets form a seal between the exhaust manifold and the cylinder head, preventing leaks. Exhaust flange gaskets seal the connection between exhaust components, such as pipes and catalytic converters. Header gaskets create a tight seal between the header and the engine block. Each type of gasket plays a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and performance of the exhaust system in vehicles.</p></p>
