<p><p>Fire Resistant Hydraulic Fluid for Metallurgy is available in four main types - HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are oil-in-water emulsions, HFB are water-in-oil emulsions, HFC are water glycol fluids, and HFD are synthetic fluids. These different types offer varying levels of fire resistance and are designed for use in different operating conditions. HFA fluids are commonly used in high-temperature applications, while HFD fluids are preferred for extreme conditions where fire safety is crucial.</p></p>
