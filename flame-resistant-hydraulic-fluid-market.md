<p><p>Flame resistant hydraulic fluids are categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-based and offer good fire resistance. HFB fluids are water-in-oil emulsions, providing improved lubrication properties. HFC fluids are water-based and offer excellent fire resistance, environmental friendliness, and biodegradability. HFD fluids are synthetic esters with superior lubricating properties and biodegradability. Each type of fluid caters to specific industry needs and applications, ensuring safe and efficient hydraulic operations.</p></p>
