<p><p>Flexible fire protection sealants are important in building construction to prevent the spread of fire. There are two main types of fire protection sealants on the market: elastomeric type and intumescent type. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and can expand and contract with the building materials, providing long-lasting protection. Intumescent sealants swell when exposed to heat, forming an insulating char that helps to slow the spread of fire. Both types are essential for maintaining fire safety in buildings.</p></p>
