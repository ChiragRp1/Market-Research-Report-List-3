<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Glue-applied</li><li>Heat-Shrink and Stretch Sleeve</li><li>In-mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexography print labels are being used in various markets including pressure sensitive, glue-applied, heat-shrink and stretch sleeve, and in-mold labels. Pressure sensitive labels have an adhesive backing for easy application. Glue-applied labels require a separate adhesive for attachment. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels shrink to fit the container when heat is applied. In-mold labels are placed directly into the mold during the manufacturing process, eliminating the need for an additional step. Each type of label offers unique benefits for different packaging needs.</p></p>
