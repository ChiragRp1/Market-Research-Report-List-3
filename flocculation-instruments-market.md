<p><p>Flocculation instruments are used in water treatment to simulate the process of bringing together particles in a liquid to form larger flocs for easier removal. The three main types of flocculation instruments are Jar Test Apparatus, Pipe Flocculator, and Paddle Flocculator. </p><p>Jar Test Apparatus is used for small-scale testing of different flocculants on water samples. Pipe Flocculator is a larger-scale instrument used in industrial water treatment plants. Paddle Flocculator uses rotating paddles to mix chemicals with water for efficient flocculation.</p></p>
