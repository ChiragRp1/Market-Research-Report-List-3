<p><strong>The Fluorine-Based Grease Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perfluoropolyether Grease</li><li>Fluorosilicone Grease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorine-Based Grease market comprises two main types: Perfluoropolyether Grease and Fluorosilicone Grease. Perfluoropolyether Grease is a high-performance grease known for its superior thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low volatility. It is commonly used in extreme temperature and harsh chemical environments. On the other hand, Fluorosilicone Grease combines the properties of both fluorine and silicone to provide excellent thermal and chemical resistance. It is often used in aerospace, automotive, and electronic applications where lubrication under extreme conditions is required.</p></p>
