<p><p>Foliar fertilizers are classified into four main types based on their nutrient composition: nitrogenous fertilizers, phosphatic fertilizers, potassic fertilizers, and micronutrients fertilizers. Nitrogenous fertilizers provide plants with essential nitrogen for growth and development. Phosphatic fertilizers contain phosphorus, which is crucial for root development and energy transfer within plants. Potassic fertilizers supply potassium, promoting flowering and fruiting. Micronutrients fertilizers contain trace elements necessary for overall plant health and productivity. Each type plays a unique role in ensuring proper plant nutrition and growth.</p></p>
