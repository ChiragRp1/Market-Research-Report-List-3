<p><p>The Food Contact Paper market consists of two main types: Non-poly-coated and Poly-coated. Non-poly-coated paper is untreated and lacks a layer of polyethylene coating. It is commonly used for packaging dry food or non-greasy items. On the other hand, Poly-coated paper has a layer of polyethylene coating that provides grease resistance and prevents the transfer of liquids. This type is typically employed for packaging greasy or moist food products. Both types serve specific purposes in the food packaging industry, offering different levels of protection and functionality.</p></p>
