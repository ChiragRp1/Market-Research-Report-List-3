<p><p>Forging lubricants can be classified into three main types based on their composition and properties: soap-based lubricants, water-based lubricants, and oil-based lubricants. Soap-based lubricants are derived from natural materials and provide good lubrication during forging processes. Water-based lubricants are environmentally friendly and offer cooling properties. Oil-based lubricants are commonly used for heavy-duty forging applications due to their high lubricity and heat resistance. Each type of lubricant has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the forging process.</p></p>
