<p><p>GigE industrial cameras come in two main types based on the image sensor they use: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and consistency. On the other hand, CMOS cameras are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular for high-speed imaging and portable applications. Both types have their strengths and are utilized in various industries based on their unique capabilities.</p></p>
