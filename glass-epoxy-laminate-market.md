<p><p>Glass epoxy laminate materials come in different grades such as G10, G11, FR4, FR5, and FR6. G10 and G11 are known for their high mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties, making them suitable for applications requiring high performance under extreme conditions. FR4 is a popular grade known for its flame retardant properties, making it ideal for use in electronic circuit boards. FR5 and FR6 grades offer similar properties but are designed for specific applications requiring high thermal performance and resistance to chemicals.</p></p>
