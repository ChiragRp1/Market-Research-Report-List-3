<p><ul><li>Cup Style</li><li>Hat Style</li><li>Pancake Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Harmonic Drive Reduction Gear market offers three types of gear designs: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style gears consist of a flexible cup-shaped outer ring, an inner ring, and a wave generator that causes the deformation of the cup to produce motion. Hat Style gears also have a cup-shaped outer ring, but they feature a flat circular disc in the middle instead of an inner ring. Pancake Style gears are flatter, with a disc-shaped outer ring and a flat circular disc in the middle, offering a more compact design.</p></p>
