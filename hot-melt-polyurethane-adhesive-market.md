<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li><li>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives are pre-formulated adhesives that do not chemically react during the curing process. They are applied in liquid form and solidify upon cooling, providing fast and strong bonding for a variety of materials. </p><p>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives, on the other hand, contain chemical components that react with external factors such as moisture or heat to cure and create a bond. This type of adhesive offers increased durability and versatility for demanding applications.</p></p>
