<p><p>The household vacuum packer market primarily consists of two types: fully automated and semi-automated. Fully automated vacuum packers are designed to operate with minimal user intervention. They typically offer advanced features such as automatic bag sealing, adjustable vacuum strength, and preset settings for different food items. On the other hand, semi-automated vacuum packers require some manual input from the user. Users need to manually place the food items in the bag, adjust the vacuum strength, and manually seal the bag before initiating the vacuum process.</p></p>
