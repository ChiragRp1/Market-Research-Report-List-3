<p><p>Industrial Bluetooth market includes single-mode modules and dual-mode modules. Single-mode modules are designed for low energy consumption and are ideal for applications that require long battery life. Dual-mode modules, on the other hand, support both classic Bluetooth and Bluetooth Low Energy, offering greater flexibility in connectivity options. Single-mode modules are commonly used in devices such as sensors and beacons, while dual-mode modules are popular in devices that require both high-speed data transfer and low energy consumption.</p></p>
