<p><p>An infusion fluid holder, also referred to as an IV fluid holder, is a medical device used to hold and suspend infusion fluids, such as medications, in a safe and stable manner during intravenous therapy. This enables medical professionals to administer fluids to patients efficiently.</p><p>The infusion fluid holder market is witnessing steady growth with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 4.80% during the forecast period.
