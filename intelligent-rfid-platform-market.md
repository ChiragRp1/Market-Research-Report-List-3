<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Intelligent RFID Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF) RFID</li><li>High-Frequency (HF) RFID</li><li>Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent RFID Platform market consists of three main types: Low Frequency (LF) RFID, High-Frequency (HF) RFID, and Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID. LF RFID operates at a frequency of 125-134 kHz and is commonly used for access control systems and animal identification. HF RFID operates at a frequency of 13.56 MHz and is often used for payment systems and tracking library books. UHF RFID operates at a frequency range of 860-960 MHz and is popular for inventory management and supply chain applications due to its longer read range and faster data transfer speeds.</p></p>
