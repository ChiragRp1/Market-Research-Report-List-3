<p><p>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) is a medical imaging technique that allows visualization of the interior of blood vessels. It involves the insertion of a catheter with an ultrasound probe into the blood vessel, providing real-time, high-resolution images of the vessel walls, lumen, and plaque buildup. IVUS tools are used in the diagnosis and management of various cardiovascular diseases, such as coronary artery disease, peripheral artery disease, and venous diseases.</p><p>The global intravascular ultrasound tools market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, technological advancements in IVUS tools, and the growing geriatric population.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the development of advanced IVUS tools with improved image quality, enhanced data acquisition capabilities, and real-time 3D imaging. These advancements are expected to fuel market growth and improve clinical outcomes.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing demand for coronary and peripheral IVUS procedures, along with the expanding applications of IVUS in complex coronary interventions and intracardiac imaging, is driving market growth. Additionally, the rising awareness about the benefits of IVUS-guided procedures among healthcare professionals and patients is further contributing to market expansion.</p><p>However, the high cost of IVUS procedures and the availability of alternative diagnostic tools may hinder market growth to some extent.</p><p>In conclusion, the global intravascular ultrasound tools market is expected to grow at a healthy CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period. Advancements in technology, increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, and expanding applications of IVUS are the key factors driving this growth.</p></p>

<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound tools market includes three main types of products: IVUS consoles, IVUS catheters, and accessories. IVUS consoles are the main control units used for image processing and displaying during intravascular ultrasound procedures. IVUS catheters are the actual probes that are inserted into the blood vessels to capture detailed images of the arterial walls. Accessories include various devices and tools that are used in conjunction with IVUS consoles and catheters, such as guide wires, pressure wires, and flushing devices. These products play a crucial role in diagnosing and guiding treatment for cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
