<p><p>The Laser Brazing market encompasses two main types of welding processes: fillet welding and lap welding. Fillet welding involves joining two pieces of metal at the edges, forming a right angle or T-shape joint. On the other hand, lap welding involves overlapping two metal sheets and joining them along the overlapped area. Both types of welding processes are performed using laser technology, which offers high precision and efficiency. These methods are widely used in various industries for their ability to create strong and durable joints in a cost-effective manner.</p></p>
