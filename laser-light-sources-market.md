<p><p>The laser light sources market can be classified into three main types based on wavelength: Visible Laser Light Sources, Infrared Laser Light Sources, and UV Laser Light Sources. Visible laser light sources produce light that is visible to the human eye, typically in the range of 400 to 700 nanometers. Infrared laser light sources emit light with longer wavelengths, above the visible spectrum, ranging from 700 nanometers to 1 millimeter. UV laser light sources emit light with shorter wavelengths, below the visible spectrum, typically between 10 to 400 nanometers.</p></p>
