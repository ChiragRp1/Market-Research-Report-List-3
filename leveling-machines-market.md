<p><strong>The Leveling Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leveling machines are classified into two main types, Type I and Type II. Type I leveling machines are designed for leveling sheets of material with a constant thickness, such as metal sheets or plastic films. These machines use a set of rollers or blades to uniformly flatten the material. On the other hand, Type II leveling machines are specialized for leveling uneven surfaces, particularly for rough or undulating materials like wood or concrete. They employ additional features like adjustable height or oscillating plates to smooth out the irregularities.</p></p>
