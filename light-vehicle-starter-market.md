<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Vehicle Starter market can be classified into two main types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters operate by directly engaging the starter motor with the engine flywheel, providing quick and efficient start-up. Electromagnetic Control starters, on the other hand, use an electromagnetic solenoid to engage the starter motor with the engine, offering smoother engagement and disengagement. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the automotive industry, providing options for various vehicle manufacturers and users.</p></p>
