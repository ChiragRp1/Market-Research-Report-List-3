<p><p>The lightweight rubber conveyor belt market is segmented based on the types of rubber used. SBR (styrene-butadiene rubber) offers good abrasion resistance and is therefore suitable for heavy-duty applications. Neoprene, known for its resistance to oil and chemicals, is preferred in industries with exposure to such substances.
