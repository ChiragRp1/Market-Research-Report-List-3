<p><strong>The Lumbar Disc Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-on-metal</li><li>Metal-on-biopolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal-on-metal lumbar disc prosthesis involves using metal components for both the ball and socket of the disc replacement. This type of prosthesis is known for its durability and stability. On the other hand, metal-on-biopolymer lumbar disc prosthesis uses a metal ball and a biopolymer socket. This combination offers a more natural movement and reduced wear and tear on surrounding tissues. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the individual patient's needs and preferences.</p></p>
