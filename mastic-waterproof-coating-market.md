<p><p>Mastic waterproof coatings come in two main market types - one component and two component. One component mastic coatings are ready to use straight out of the container and do not require any mixing before application. On the other hand, two component mastic coatings consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before use. The choice between one component and two component coatings depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user, as well as the intended application of the coating.</p></p>
