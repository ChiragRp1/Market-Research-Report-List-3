Paraffin-based white oils are derived from petroleum and are known for their stability and low volatility. Cycloalkyl-based white oils, on the other hand, are produced from cyclic hydrocarbons and are favored for their high thermal stability and oxidation resistance. Both types of white oils have specific applications and benefits in various industries.</p></p>
