<p><p>Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) panels are categorized into two main types - Type I and Type II based on their physical and mechanical properties. Type I MDF panels are designed for use in interior applications where moisture exposure is low, while Type II panels are engineered for higher moisture resistance, making them suitable for use in potentially damp environments such as kitchens and bathrooms. Both types offer a smooth surface for finishing and are commonly used in furniture, cabinets, and construction projects.</p></p>
