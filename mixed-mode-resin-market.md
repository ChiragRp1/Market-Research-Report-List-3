Hydrophobic resins are used for the purification of proteins and other biomolecules based on hydrophobic interactions. Another type is Hydroxyapatite resins, which are used for the purification of biomolecules based on both ionic and hydrophobic interactions. These resins provide a versatile approach for the purification of various biomolecules in bioprocessing and biopharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
