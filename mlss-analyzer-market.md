<p><ul><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual Channel MLSS analyzers have two separate measuring channels for monitoring MLSS (Mixed Liquor Suspended Solids) levels in wastewater treatment processes. This allows for more accurate and reliable readings. On the other hand, Multi Channel MLSS analyzers have multiple measuring channels, typically four or more, which can be used to monitor MLSS levels at various points within a treatment plant. This provides a more comprehensive view of the process and helps in optimizing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
