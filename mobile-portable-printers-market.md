<p><p>Mobile/portable printers come in three main types in the market: thermal printers, inkjet printers, and impact printers. Thermal printers use heat to transfer ink onto paper, making them fast and quiet. Inkjet printers spray liquid ink onto paper, producing high-quality images and text. Impact printers use a print head to physically strike an inked ribbon, creating characters on the paper. Each type offers unique benefits for different printing needs in portable and mobile settings.</p></p>
