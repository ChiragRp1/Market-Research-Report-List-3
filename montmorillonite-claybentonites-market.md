<p><p>Montmorillonite clay, also known as Bentonite, is available in three main types based on the predominant cation present in the clay structure: Sodium Bentonite, Calcium Bentonite, and Potassium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite is highly swelling and has a wide range of applications. Calcium Bentonite is known for its absorption properties and is commonly used in beauty and skincare products. Potassium Bentonite is less commonly found and is typically used in agriculture and animal feed applications. Each type offers unique benefits and properties for different industries.</p></p>
