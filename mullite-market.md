<p><p>Mullite is a type of ceramic material with strong heat resistance and excellent mechanical properties. The Mullite market comprises two main types: natural Mullite and fused Mullite. Natural Mullite is derived from aluminum silicates found in nature and is primarily used in the refractory industry for lining furnaces and kilns. Fused Mullite, on the other hand, is produced through a high-temperature fusion process and is widely used in industries such as steel, ceramics, and foundries for applications requiring high strength and thermal shock resistance.</p></p>
