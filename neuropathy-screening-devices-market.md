<p><p>Neuropathy screening devices are divided into two main types: Non-Electronic Devices and Electronic Devices. Non-Electronic Devices include tools such as monofilaments and tuning forks that assess sensory perception and nerve function through touch and pressure. On the other hand, Electronic Devices utilize advanced technologies like nerve conduction studies and electromyography to measure nerve activity and diagnose neuropathy more precisely. Both types play a crucial role in early detection and monitoring of neuropathic conditions.</p></p>
