<p><p>Nonwoven adhesive for personal hygiene products market includes two main types: hot melt adhesive and water-based adhesives. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that are melted to apply and solidify upon cooling, providing strong and flexible bonds. Water-based adhesives, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly and contain water as a carrier, making them safer for personal hygiene products. Both types of adhesives are essential in manufacturing products like diapers, sanitary pads, and incontinence products.</p></p>
