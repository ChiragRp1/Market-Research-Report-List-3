<p><p>Occupancy monitoring market types include Microwave Sensors, Infrared Time-of-Flight Sensors, Ultrasonic Sensors, and Others. Microwave sensors use radio waves to detect motion, while Infrared Time-of-Flight sensors measure distance by emitting pulses of light. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to determine occupancy. Other types may include pressure sensors or camera-based systems. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages in monitoring occupancy levels in various settings such as offices, retail spaces, and public buildings.</p></p>
