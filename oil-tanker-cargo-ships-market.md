<p><strong>The Oil Tanker Cargo Ships Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VLCC</li><li>ULCC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil tanker cargo ships are categorized into VLCC (Very Large Crude Carriers) and ULCC (Ultra Large Crude Carriers) based on their sizes and capacities. VLCCs typically have a capacity between 200,000 and 320,000 deadweight tons, while ULCCs exceed 320,000 deadweight tons. VLCCs dominate the crude oil transportation market due to their versatility in carrying various grades of crude oil and their ability to navigate most ports. On the other hand, ULCCs are utilized for long-haul voyages where large quantities of oil need to be transported.</p></p>
