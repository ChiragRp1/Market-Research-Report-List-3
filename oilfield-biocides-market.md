<p><p>Oilfield biocides are chemicals used to control microbial growth in oil and gas production facilities. The main types of oilfield biocides are Glutaraldehyde, DBNPA, THPS, Chlorine, and Quaternary Ammonium. Glutaraldehyde is effective against a broad spectrum of microorganisms. DBNPA is fast-acting and long-lasting. THPS is non-oxidizing and suitable for high-temperature applications. Chlorine is a traditional biocide with rapid kill rates. Quaternary Ammonium compounds are effective against biofilms. Each type of biocide offers unique advantages for controlling microbial growth in oilfield operations.</p></p>
