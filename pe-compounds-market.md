<p><p>PE compounds market can be classified into three main types: HDPE type, XLPE type, and Other market. HDPE type compounds are high-density polyethylene materials known for their excellent strength and impact resistance, making them suitable for various applications such as bottles, pipes, and packaging. XLPE type compounds, on the other hand, are cross-linked polyethylene materials that offer superior thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for use in cables and wires. The Other market comprises a variety of PE compounds with unique properties and applications, catering to diverse industries and requirements.</p></p>
