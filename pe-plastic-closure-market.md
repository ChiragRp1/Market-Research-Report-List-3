<p><p>PE Plastic Closure Market includes two main types - Compression Moulding and Injection Molding. Compression Moulding involves placing preheated plastic material into a mold cavity and then applying pressure to create the desired shape. Injection Molding, on the other hand, involves injecting molten plastic material into a mold cavity under high pressure. Both methods are widely used in the manufacturing of PE plastic closures for various industries due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
