<p><p>Piezoceramic actuators market is segmented into different types like Multilayer Actuators, Stacked Actuators, Shear Actuators, and Amplified Actuators. Multilayer actuators consist of multiple layers of piezoceramic materials stacked together to increase displacement. Stacked actuators are made by stacking multiple single-layer actuators to achieve higher forces. Shear actuators generate motion by shearing the piezoceramic material. Amplified actuators use mechanical amplification to increase the output displacement. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements in industries like robotics, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>
