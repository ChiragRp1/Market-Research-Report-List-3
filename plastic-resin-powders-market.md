<p><p>Plastic resin powders are categorized based on their density. Resins with a density of less than 1 g/cm³ are lightweight and often used for applications where weight is a concern. Those with a density of 1 g/cm³ to 10 g/cm³ are considered medium-density resins and are versatile in various industries. Resins with a density of more than 10 g/cm³ are high-density resins, offering strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Each type of resin powder caters to specific needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
