<p><strong>The Platform Screen Door System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Height</li><li>Half Height</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platform screen door systems are commonly divided into two main types based on their height: full height and half height. Full-height platform screen doors extend from the floor to the ceiling, providing a complete barrier between the platform and the tracks. Half-height platform screen doors cover only a portion of the platform's height, typically stopping at waist or chest level. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with full-height doors offering greater protection and security, while half-height doors may be more cost-effective and suitable for certain environments.</p></p>
