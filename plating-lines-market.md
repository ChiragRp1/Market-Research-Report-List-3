<p><p>Plating lines refer to the equipment used in electroplating processes, which involve depositing a thin layer of metal onto a surface. There are three main types of plating lines: rack plating lines, barrel plating lines, and other specialized plating lines. Rack plating lines are used for parts that require individual handling and precise control over the plating process. Barrel plating lines are designed for mass production of small parts by placing them in a rotating barrel with the plating solution. Other specialized plating lines cater to unique requirements in the industry.</p></p>
