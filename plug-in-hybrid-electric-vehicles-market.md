<p><p>Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) can be categorized into two market types based on how they generate electricity. The first type is the stored electricity market, where the vehicle relies primarily on a battery pack that is charged by plugging into an external power source. The second type is the On Board Electric Generator market, in which the vehicle has an internal combustion engine that acts as a generator to charge the battery while driving, providing extended range and flexibility for drivers.</p></p>
