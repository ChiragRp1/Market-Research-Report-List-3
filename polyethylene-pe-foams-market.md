<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) foams are categorized into two main types: Non-crosslinked polyethylene (Non-XLPE) foam and crosslinked polyethylene (XLPE) foam. Non-XLPE foam is known for its excellent cushioning, shock absorption, and insulation properties, making it ideal for use in packaging, automotive interiors, and construction. XLPE foam, on the other hand, has superior mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making it suitable for more demanding applications such as marine flotation, medical packaging, and industrial insulation. Both types of PE foams provide lightweight, cost-effective solutions for various industries.</p></p>
