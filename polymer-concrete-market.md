<p><strong>The Polymer Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Modified Concrete (PMC)</li><li>Polymer Resin Concrete (PC)</li><li>Polymer Impregnated Concrete (PIC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer Concrete is a type of concrete that uses polymers as a binding agent in place of traditional cement. Polymer Modified Concrete (PMC) is a type of polymer concrete where polymers are added to the concrete mix to enhance properties like strength and durability. Polymer Resin Concrete (PC) uses polymer resins as a binding agent to improve chemical resistance and reduce water absorption. Polymer Impregnated Concrete (PIC) involves impregnating polymers into the concrete matrix to improve toughness and durability.</p></p>
