<p><p>Polystyrene film is categorized into three main types based on thickness: below 50μm, 50-100μm, and above 100μm. Films below 50μm are typically used in packaging applications where a thin barrier is required. Films with a thickness between 50-100μm are commonly used for wrapping and laminating purposes. Films with a thickness above 100μm are utilized in heavy-duty applications such as industrial and construction sectors where durability and strength are crucial. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
