<p><p>A Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) is a handheld device used to test and analyze the performance of optical fibers in communication networks. It works by sending a pulse of light down the fiber and measuring the reflections and losses to identify any faults or issues in the network. OTDRs are essential tools for maintaining and troubleshooting fiber optic networks, as they can pinpoint the exact location of a problem quickly and accurately.</p><p>The Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-speed data transmission and the expansion of fiber optic networks worldwide.
