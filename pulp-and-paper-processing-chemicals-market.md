Kraft Pulping involves the use of a strong alkaline solution to break down wood fibers and remove lignin. Chemical Pulping utilizes various chemicals to separate cellulose fibers from wood. The Other market includes specialty chemicals used in processes such as bleaching, sizing, and coating. Each type of market plays a vital role in the production of high-quality pulp and paper products.</p></p>
