<p><p>Pump combiners in fiber laser machines are essential components that combine multiple pump laser signals into a single output. There are two main types of pump combiners on the market: multimode and single-mode. Multimode pump combiners are designed for applications where multiple pump sources with different wavelengths are used. Single-mode pump combiners are suited for applications where a single pump source with a specific wavelength is required. Both types play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and performance of fiber laser machines.</p></p>
