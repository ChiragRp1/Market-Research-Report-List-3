<p><p>Radiant electric floor heating refers to a heating system that involves electric cables or mats installed beneath flooring to provide warmth. This system falls under the Electric market type, which utilizes electricity to generate heat. On the other hand, Hydronic floor heating involves circulating warm water through tubing under the floor, falling under the Hydronic market type. Both systems offer energy-efficient and even heating, with electric floor heating being easier to install and more suitable for smaller spaces, while hydronic floor heating is ideal for larger areas and provides longer-lasting heat.</p></p>
