<p><p>Radio Frequency (RF) Testing Market Types:</p><p>Over-the-Air Testing: Over-the-Air Testing involves the evaluation of wireless devices and systems in real-world scenarios, such as houses, offices, or vehicles, using various test methodologies. It assesses the device's performance, signal strength, data transfer rate, and interference susceptibility, ensuring it meets regulatory and industry standards.</p><p>Specific Absorption Rate (SAR) Testing: SAR Testing measures the amount of radio frequency energy absorbed by the human body when using wireless devices. It determines if a device's electromagnetic exposure level complies with safety guidelines, protecting users from potential health risks associated with excessive radio frequency absorption. SAR testing considers factors like device configuration, usage modes, and proximity to the human body to assess the potential bioeffects of RF exposure.</p></p>
