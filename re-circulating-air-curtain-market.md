<p><strong>The Re-Circulating Air Curtain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HorizontalInstallation</li><li>VerticalInstallation</li><li>Recessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Re-circulating air curtains are available in three market types: Horizontal Installation, Vertical Installation, and Recessed Installation. Horizontal Installation air curtains are designed to be mounted above doorways, blowing air horizontally across the entrance. Vertical Installation air curtains are mounted on the side of doorways, blowing air vertically across the entrance. Recessed Installation air curtains are integrated into the ceiling or walls, providing a more discreet option for controlling air flow. Each type offers different solutions for maintaining indoor temperature and air quality.</p></p>
