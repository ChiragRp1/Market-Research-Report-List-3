<p><strong>The Reheating Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Walking Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Pusher Furnaces</li><li>Walking-Beam Furnaces</li><li>Refractory Beam Furnace</li><li>Tunnel Furnaces</li><li>Rotary Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Rototherm Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reheating furnaces are used in various industries for heating metal ingots or billets before further processing. The market types include walking hearth furnaces, pusher furnaces, walking-beam furnaces, refractory beam furnace, tunnel furnaces, rotary hearth furnaces, and rototherm furnaces. Walking hearth furnaces have a moving hearth for heating large quantities of metal, while pusher furnaces push material through a heated chamber. Walking-beam furnaces use beams to convey material for heating, and refractory beam furnaces have high efficiency due to their construction. Tunnel furnaces are long chambers where material is heated as it passes through. Rotary hearth furnaces rotate material for even heating, and rototherm furnaces offer precise temperature control.</p></p>
