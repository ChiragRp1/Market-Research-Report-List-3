<p><strong>The Satellite Enabled IoT Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L Band</li><li>Ku-Band</li><li>Ka-Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Satellite Enabled IoT Software market comprises three types: L Band, Ku-Band, and Ka-Band. L Band refers to the frequency range between 1 and 2 GHz and is primarily used for global satellite communications. Ku-Band refers to higher frequencies between 12 and 18 GHz and is commonly used for satellite broadcasting and broadband internet. Lastly, Ka-Band operates at higher frequencies between 26 and 40 GHz, offering even higher data rates suitable for applications like high-speed internet access and advanced satellite communication systems. These market segments define the different frequency bands in which IoT devices can communicate via satellites.</p></p>
