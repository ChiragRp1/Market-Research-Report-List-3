<p><ul><li>Powder Form</li><li>Liquid Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seed treatment materials market offers two main types: powder form and liquid form. Powder form materials are finely ground substances that are easily soluble in water and can be applied to seeds either by coating or dusting. They provide convenience in handling and allow for precise dosing. On the other hand, liquid form materials are solutions that can be directly applied to seeds or used for seed treatment processes. They offer good coverage and can penetrate seed surfaces effectively. Both forms contribute to the effective protection and enhancement of seed quality, resulting in improved plant growth and productivity.</p></p>
