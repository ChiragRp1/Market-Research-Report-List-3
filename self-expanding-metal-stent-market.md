<p><p>The Self-Expanding Metal Stent market consists of three main types: Fully Covered, Partially Covered, and Uncovered. Fully Covered stents have a protective layer that completely encases the stent, providing full coverage. Partially Covered stents have a combination of covered and uncovered sections, allowing for targeted treatment. Uncovered stents have no protective layer, allowing for direct contact between the stent and the affected area. Each type of stent offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the location of the blockage.</p></p>
