<p><p>Semiconductor rectifiers are categorized into three main types: LCR (Low Current Rectifiers), MCR (Medium Current Rectifiers), and HCR (High Current Rectifiers). LCR rectifiers are used for low current applications such as consumer electronics. MCR rectifiers are suitable for medium current applications like industrial machinery. HCR rectifiers are designed for high current applications including power distribution systems. Each type of rectifier is tailored to handle specific current levels and is chosen based on the requirements of the electronic device or system being used.</p></p>
