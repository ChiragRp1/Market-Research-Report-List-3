<p><ul><li>Regular EVA</li><li>Anti-PID EVA</li><li>White EVA</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular EVA film is the standard encapsulant used in solar panels, providing strong adhesion and UV resistance. Anti-PID EVA film is designed to prevent potential-induced degradation in solar panels, enhancing their lifespan. White EVA film is used for aesthetics and to reduce heat absorption, improving overall efficiency. Other market types may include ultra-clear EVA for higher light transmission, fast-curing EVA for increased production speed, or low-temperature EVA for cold climate applications. Each type of EVA film offers unique benefits to suit different requirements in the solar industry.</p></p>
