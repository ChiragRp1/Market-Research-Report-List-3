<p><p>Stain removers are commonly available in two main types: liquid and solid. They are convenient and easy to use for a wide range of fabric types. On the other hand, solid stain removers come in the form of powders, sticks, or wipes, offering a more targeted approach for specific types of stains. They are often preferred for their portability and ease of storage. Both liquid and solid stain removers cater to different consumer preferences and cleaning needs.</p></p>
