<p><p>Strain sensors are classified into two main types: ordinary strain sensors and reusable strain sensors. Ordinary strain sensors are designed for single-use applications and are typically disposed of after one-time use. In contrast, reusable strain sensors can be used multiple times, making them more cost-effective and environmentally friendly. Both types of sensors serve the purpose of measuring the amount of strain or deformation in a material, helping to monitor structural integrity or performance in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and civil engineering.</p></p>
