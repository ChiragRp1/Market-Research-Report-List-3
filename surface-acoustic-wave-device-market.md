<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) devices are classified into various types including filters, oscillators, resonators, transducers, and others. Filters are used to selectively allow certain frequencies to pass through while blocking others. Oscillators generate precise and stable frequencies for various applications. Resonators are used to enhance the performance of electronic circuits. Transducers convert energy from one form to another. Other types of SAW devices include delay lines, sensors, and actuators, which have various specialized functions in electronic systems.</p></p>
