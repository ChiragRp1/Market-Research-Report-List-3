<p><p>The Surgery Robotic Market encompasses two main types of systems: Multi-port Robotic System and Single-port Robotic System. The Multi-port Robotic System involves the use of multiple robotic arms, each inserted through separate incisions, allowing for more complex and precise surgical procedures. On the other hand, the Single-port Robotic System utilizes a single incision, reducing the invasiveness of the surgery and potentially speeding up recovery time. Both types aim to enhance surgical precision, decrease complications, and improve patient outcomes in a variety of medical procedures.</p></p>
