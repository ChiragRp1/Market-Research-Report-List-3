<p><p>The Surgical Ultrasonic Generator market is divided into two types: Single-frequency and Multi-frequency. In the Single-frequency market, generators produce ultrasonic waves at a fixed frequency, typically ranging from 20 kHz to 60 kHz. They are widely used for specific surgical procedures due to their precise and controlled energy delivery. On the other hand, Multi-frequency generators generate ultrasonic waves at different frequencies, allowing surgeons to choose the most suitable frequency for different procedures. This flexibility enhances their versatility and adaptability in various surgical applications.</p></p>
