<p><p>Gear driven transfer cases use a set of gears to transfer power from the transmission to the front and rear axles of an SUV. They are known for their durability and ability to handle high torque. Both types of transfer cases have their own advantages and are used in different SUV models depending on the desired performance and driving preferences.</p></p>
