<p><p>Thermistors are electronic devices used for temperature measurement and control. They are classified into three main market types: Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC), Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC), and Combination Temperature Resistant (CTR). PTC thermistors have a resistance that increases with temperature, making them suitable for overcurrent protection. NTC thermistors have a resistance that decreases with temperature, enabling their use in temperature sensing applications. CTR thermistors combine the characteristics of both PTC and NTC thermistors, offering a versatile range of temperature measurement and control functionalities.</p></p>
