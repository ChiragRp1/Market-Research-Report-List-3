<p><p>Thermochromatic inks are divided into three main market types based on their color-changing properties. Color visible type inks change color visibly when exposed to heat, making them suitable for applications like temperature-sensitive labels. Color invisible type inks remain invisible until heated, revealing a hidden message or design. Color change type inks transition between two or more colors based on temperature fluctuations, offering dynamic and eye-catching effects. Each type of thermochromatic ink serves different purposes and can be utilized in various industries for innovative and interactive products.</p></p>
