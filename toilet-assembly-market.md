<p><p>Toilet assembly markets are dominated by three main types: strong fall type, siphon type, and others. The strong fall type toilets have a steeper angle in the trapway, allowing for more forceful waste removal. Siphon type toilets use a siphoning action to pull waste down the drain. The "others" category includes various alternative designs and technologies, such as pressure-assisted toilets. Each type has its own advantages and may appeal to different consumers based on preferences for flushing power, water efficiency, or ease of maintenance.</p></p>
