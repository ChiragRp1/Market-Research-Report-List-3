<p><p>Tonneau covers in the market are available in two main types, soft rolling and hard folding. Soft rolling tonneau covers are made of vinyl or fabric materials that can be easily rolled up to access the truck bed. On the other hand, hard folding tonneau covers are constructed with sturdy materials like aluminum or fiberglass and have multiple panels that can be folded up for bed access. Both types provide security and weather protection for items in the truck bed while offering different levels of convenience and durability.</p></p>
