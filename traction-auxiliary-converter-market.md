<p><p>Traction auxiliary converters in the market can be classified into two types: Integrated and Detached. Integrated converters are built into the traction system itself, providing a more compact and streamlined solution. On the other hand, detached converters are separate units that can be installed independently, offering more flexibility in terms of placement and maintenance. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on factors such as system requirements, space constraints, and cost considerations.</p></p>
