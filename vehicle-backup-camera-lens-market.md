<p><p>The Vehicle Backup Camera Lens market includes two main types: CCD Cameras and CMOS Cameras. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras are known for their high-quality image resolution, making them ideal for professional and high-end vehicles. On the other hand, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are more cost-effective and energy-efficient, making them popular for use in mainstream and entry-level vehicles. Both types offer rearview assistance for drivers, helping to improve safety and prevent accidents while backing up.</p></p>
