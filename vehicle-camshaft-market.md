<p><p>The vehicle camshaft market consists of three main types: cast camshaft, assembled camshaft, and forged camshaft. Assembled camshafts are created by assembling different components together, providing flexibility in design. Forged camshafts are made by shaping metal under high pressure, resulting in a strong and durable option. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
