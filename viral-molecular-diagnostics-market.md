<p><p>Viral molecular diagnostics refer to the use of molecular biology techniques to identify and detect viral infections. This methodology is widely used in clinical laboratories to diagnose viral infections accurately.</p><p>The viral molecular diagnostics market has been experiencing substantial growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory. The market growth is primarily driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of viral infections, advancements in molecular diagnostic technologies, and the growing demand for accurate and rapid diagnostics.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has significantly contributed to the growth of the viral molecular diagnostics market.
