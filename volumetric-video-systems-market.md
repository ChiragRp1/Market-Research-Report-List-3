<p><p>Volumetric video systems are advanced technologies that enable the capture and representation of three-dimensional (3D) objects or subjects in a realistic manner. Volumetric video is often used in industries such as entertainment, gaming, education, healthcare, and communication, as it offers immersive and interactive experiences.</p><p>The market for volumetric video systems has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for high-quality visual content, rising adoption of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies, and advancements in camera technologies are some of the key factors driving market growth.
