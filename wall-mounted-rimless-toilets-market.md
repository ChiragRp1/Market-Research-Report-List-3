<p><p>Wall mounted rimless toilets come in two market types: monolithic and separate. </p><p>Monolithic toilets have a seamless and integrated design, where the toilet bowl and tank are combined into one unit for a sleek and modern look. They are popular for their space-saving design and easy cleaning.</p><p>Separate toilets have a more traditional design, with a separate tank and bowl. They offer more customization options and are typically easier to install and maintain. Both types offer the benefits of rimless technology for improved hygiene and easier cleaning.</p></p>
