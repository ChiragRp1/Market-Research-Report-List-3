<p><p>Waveguide components and assemblies market includes adapters, couplers, isolators and circulators, duplexers, pressure windows, and others. Adapters are used to join waveguides with different sizes or shapes. Couplers split power into two parts or combine two signals into one. Isolators and circulators are crucial for signal direction control and isolation. Duplexers allow transmission and reception on a single antenna. Pressure windows protect waveguides in high-pressure environments. Other components may include terminations, filters, and transitions for specific applications.</p></p>
